Tournament App Basic-

Add a set of players
Randomly generate groupings of two for first round
Calculate how many rounds needed to get to a winner and represent them.
Display matchups
Take score inputs
Progress winning players to future rounds
Assume max 16 players.


extras -
Display scores
player stats? e.g. point win percentage
if uneven number of players, be able to select a bi for one player to round 2.




Database columns

id, playername, round 1 win, round 2 win, round 3 win, round 4 win, total points won , total points lost.
